About Me

I am a Lead Game Designer with 13 years of experience in the industry. Game design, for me, is the artful creation of systems that transport players into enchanting worlds.

Over the years, I shipped three titles, each a testament to my unwavering commitment to delivering immersive and captivating experiences. From concept to launch, I thrive on challenges and victories, relishing the joy of bringing dreams to millions of players.

Beyond development, I have a particular fondness for multiplayer RPGs. Exploring these digital landscapes allows me to revel in shared narratives and the unique camaraderie this genre offers.

Let's work together to craft experiences that transcend the boundaries of pixels.

Nintendo 64 - Wedding Gift

When my best buddy got married I wanted to gift something special. We used to play Nintendo 64 games all the time and I wanted to call back to these memories. Thus this hobbyist project was born: Create a custom N64 game that runs on the original hardware (homebrew). Little did I know that this project would end up taking more than a year and teach me some incredible lessons along the way:
• Blender low poly modeling and texturing
• N64 library and tool chain on WSL, programming in C.
• And finally getting the project on an actual N64 cartridge.
