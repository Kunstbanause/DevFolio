About Me

For almost 15 years I have always worked to create safe and fun online spaces for parties, friends, family and strangers alike. For this I am rewarded every day, as millions of players enjoy my games all around the world.

I understand game design as the careful creation of interconnected systems to create fun, challenging, emotional, engaging and satisfying experiences.

I have touched most aspects of game design from system design, progression, motivation and game loops, content creation to Character, Camera & Controls. My strong points lie in combat, player ability & enemy design, as well as multiplayer design, Balancing and data creation & maintenance.

I have shipped multiple projects and accompanied them from the earliest concept stages, through pitching internally as well as externally, presenting to press at conventions and online, until finally releasing into early access and in full. Furthermore providing live service with updates, DLC and Expansions

I am a strong problem solver and I belive in the strength in compromises and shared, cross department accomplimshemnts. I build the design department from the ground up and lead young to senior designers.

Working with internal propriatary tools I was able to greatly influence the workflow and prosess for the design from concepting, to implementation and iteration.
